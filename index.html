About DEVAM

The story behind India's trusted welding machine manufacturer.

Our Vision

Since its inception, DEVAM has been driven by a singular vision: to be the **most trusted** name in welding technology, delivering machines that combine **superior performance** with **unbeatable reliability**. We cater to a diverse clientele, from small fabrication shops to heavy industrial manufacturers.

Engineering Excellence

Our machines are built on the latest **Inverter PWM technology** combined with an **Active Feedback Control System**. This ensures stable arc performance, high energy efficiency, and ease of maintenance. Every DEVAM machine undergoes rigorous testing to guarantee it meets the highest standards of industrial demand.

Customer Focus

We are not just selling welding machines; we are providing a complete solution. Our commitment extends to providing **exceptional service support** and quick access to genuine **spares backup**, minimizing downtime and maximizing your productivity.
